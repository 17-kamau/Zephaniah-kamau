Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about football rules, leagues, players, and more.

How long is a football match?+
A standard football match lasts 90 minutes, split into two 45-minute halves, plus stoppage time.
What is the offside rule?+
A player is offside if they are nearer to the opponent's goal than both the ball and the second-last defender when the ball is played to them, unless they're in their own half.
How many players are on a football team?+
Each team starts with 11 players: 10 outfield players and 1 goalkeeper.
What are the major football leagues in the world?+
Some major leagues include the English Premier League, La Liga, Serie A, Bundesliga, and Ligue 1.
Can matches end in a draw?+
Yes, league matches can end in a draw. Knockout games may go to extra time or penalties.
What are yellow and red cards?+
A yellow card is a warning for bad behavior. A red card means a player is sent off and cannot be replaced.
